# Hehner Predicate Grammar for UFL

> Status: stub. Filled by spec 0005. Resolves open question Q2 (predicate
> completeness — can timing/resource constraints be expressed as predicates?).

The Hehner predicative grammar for UFL expressions: predicates over pre-state
`x` and post-state `x'`, with `P(x, x') ∈ {T, F}`.

- Sequential composition = conjunction.
- Parallel composition = existential quantification.
